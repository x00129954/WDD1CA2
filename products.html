Placing an order

If you wish to place an order with Massive Munchies please visit our Contact Us page and fill out or short form. In the comments section please enter a list of the candy you wish to purchase followed by the weight. e.g.

Cola Bottle 100g

Milk Teeth 3kg

etc...

Once you have completed your order be sure to hit Submit and we will work on sending you your munchie goodness as soon as possible.
